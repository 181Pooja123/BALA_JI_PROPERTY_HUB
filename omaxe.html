Omaxe World Street is one of the most iconic commercial destinations in Faridabad, inspired by the world's famous streets such as London, Paris, Amsterdam, Athens, and San Francisco. Developed by Omaxe Ltd., this project combines global architecture with modern business opportunities, making it a perfect investment choice for entrepreneurs, retailers, and investors alike.

Located in Sector 79, Faridabad, Omaxe World Street is strategically positioned with excellent connectivity to Delhi, Gurugram, and Noida via the Delhi-Mathura Road and upcoming metro extensions. The development features a mix of retail shops, office spaces, food courts, entertainment zones, and lifestyle hubs — creating a vibrant ecosystem for business and leisure.

Why Invest in Omaxe World Street?

Omaxe World Street stands out as a premier commercial hub in Faridabad, offering a high footfall location surrounded by dense residential areas and reputed educational institutions. Designed with a global theme inspired by iconic world cities, it creates an aesthetic and vibrant atmosphere that attracts both visitors and investors.

With assured rental potential, it serves as an ideal destination for brands, startups, cafés, and showrooms. Backed by Omaxe Ltd.'s strong reputation and proven legacy in real estate, the project is strategically positioned in a future growth zone, as Faridabad rapidly evolves into NCR's next major commercial hotspot.
